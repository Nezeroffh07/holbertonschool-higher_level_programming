The ``5-text_indentation`` module
=================================

Using ``text_indentation``
--------------------------

    >>> text_indentation = __import__('5-text_indentation').text_indentation

Normal cases:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello. How are you? I am fine: great.")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    How are you?
    <BLANKLINE>
    I am fine:
    <BLANKLINE>
    great.
    <BLANKLINE>

    >>> text_indentation("   Spaces before.   And after?  Yes:  ")
    Spaces before.
    <BLANKLINE>
    And after?
    <BLANKLINE>
    Yes:
    <BLANKLINE>

    >>> text_indentation("No punctuation here")
    No punctuation here
